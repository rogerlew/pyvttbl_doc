Correlation¶

Correlation is capable of performing pearson, spearman, kendalltau, or pointbiserial correlations between multiple variables. Printing the object will output coefficients, and non-familywise error corrected 2-tail p-values. Larzelere and Mulaik step down Significance testing is also performed.
